ABOUT KINOCEAN

Welcome to the Kinocean Boat

Your #1 Welded Aluminum Boat Builders for Pontoon Boat, Jon Boat, Utility Boat and Yamaha Marine Outboards.

Located in Foshan City, Guangdong Province, China. We have specialized in this area for more than 14 years, main business is water sport products. With more than 400 staffs and three workshops.

We have a modern production facility covering over 60,000 square meters, following a fully vertically integrated production model — from the hull structure and decking to interior seating and key components, the majority of boats are meticulously built in-house at own facility.

By overseeing every detail through internal team, maintain strict quality control at every stage, ensuring exceptional consistency, durability, and craftsmanship in every vessel.

PROVEN QUALITY

Built To Last

Built To Last

Hulls are all welded-hull seams, bulkheads, stringer, transom and gunnels are joined to form one unitized system stronger than the individual parts.

Selected Materials

Crafted from the strongest marine-grade aluminum: 5052 H34 alloy, enhanced with magnesium for superior corrosion resistance.

Premium Coat

Multi-layer powder-coat finish that is three times harder, up to four times thicker and keeps its color up to 20 times longer than traditional liquid paint.
